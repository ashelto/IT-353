This is the screen when you first open up the web page. 
 

When you select an answer and click submit on the question the question will fade away and you will be notified whether or not your answer was correct.
The progress bar at the top shows how many questions (out of 9) you have answered. 
(in this example I answered question 1 correctly)
 

Answering a question incorrectly (or leaving it blank) will notify the user that their answer was incorrect, the correct answer, and where to find information 
about the question. 
(in this screen shot I answered question 2 incorrectly. note how to progress bar incremented to 2)
 

I have completed the quiz and have received appropiate feedback from my answers, now I can submit the quiz by clicking the "submit" button in the upper left hand corner. 
 

After I click the submit button the screen fades to black and my score and my grade are displayed on the screen. I can exit out of this screen and restart 
the quiz by clicking the "restart" button in the top left corner. You can click the restart button at any time to restart your progress
